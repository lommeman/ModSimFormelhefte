\subsection{Sphere}
Formulas from spherical coordinates \((r, \theta, \phi)\) to Cartesian coordinates \((x, y, z)\):

\begin{align*}
x &= r \sin\phi \cos\theta \\
y &= r \sin\phi \sin\theta \\
z &= r \cos\phi
\end{align*}
